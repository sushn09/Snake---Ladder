Snakes & Ladders
Tutorial

Roll the dice and your counter will move forward the number of spaces shown on the dice.

Roll the dice and if your counter lands at the bottom of a ladder, your counter will move up to the top of the ladder.

Roll the dice and if your counter lands on the head of a snake, your counter will slide down to the bottom of the snake

The first player to get to the space that says '100' is the winner.
